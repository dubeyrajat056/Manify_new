How Manify Works for Companies

A simple step-by-step process designed to make purchasing smarter and faster.

1

Register Your Company

Create your business account and complete profile verification to access trusted vendors and sourcing tools.

2

Search Products or Vendors

Use advanced filters to find suppliers based on product type, category, location, price range, certifications, and business needs.

3

Raise Requirement / Request Quotation

Need a product? Send a quotation request directly for listed products or create a custom requirement.

4

Receive Multiple Quotations

Vendors submit offers with pricing, quantity, timelines, and commercial terms.

5

Compare & Approve

Review all quotations side by side and select the best option based on price, quality, and delivery commitments.

6

Confirm Order

Once approved, the quotation is converted into a confirmed order through the platform.
