Vendor Requirements & Standards

To maintain professional standards on the platform, vendors are expected to meet quality, service, and business reliability benchmarks.

Quality Standards

Vendors should maintain consistent product quality and dependable service levels. We encourage:

  • Accurate product information
  • Timely order fulfillment
  • Professional communication
  • Consistent product standards
  • Positive customer experience
  • Reliable after-sales support

Verification Process

All vendors go through a structured onboarding process that may include:

  • Business registration validation
  • Identity verification
  • Product or service capability review
  • Operational assessment
  • Documentation checks
  • Profile authenticity review
