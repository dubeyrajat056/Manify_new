Vendor Requirements & Standards

To maintain the highest quality standards on our platform, we require all vendors to meet specific criteria and maintain consistent performance levels.

Quality Standards

Vendors must demonstrate consistent quality in their products and maintain high standards of production and service delivery. We verify quality through:

  • Regular product testing and quality audits
  • Certification verification (ISO, CE, UL, etc.)
  • Customer satisfaction metrics and reviews
  • Production capacity and capability assessments

Verification Process

All vendors undergo a comprehensive verification process including:

  • Business registration and legal entity validation
  • Identity verification for key personnel
  • Financial stability assessment
  • Production facility inspection (when applicable)
  • Quality management system evaluation
